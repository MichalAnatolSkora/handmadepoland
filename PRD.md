# Handmade Poland — Product Requirements Document

## Overview

Handmade Poland is an online marketplace for handmade craftsman goods made in Poland. We connect artisans with buyers, allowing craftsmen to list and sell their handmade products.

## Business Model

- **Listing is free** — sellers can publish their items at no cost.
- **Revenue from transactions** — we earn a margin on each completed sale.
- No subscription fees, no listing fees.

## Core Concept

- All products sold on the platform must be **handmade**.
- Sellers (craftsmen) create listings with descriptions, photos, and pricing.
- Buyers browse, search, and purchase items.
- The platform handles the transaction and takes a commission.

## Key Stakeholders

- **Sellers** — independent craftsmen and artisans publishing their handmade goods.
- **Buyers** — customers looking for unique, handmade products from Poland.
